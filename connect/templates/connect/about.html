Features

  • BITS Line

    BITS Pilani's very own Skype where you can Connect with fellow BITSians, free of cost just using just your BITS ID! Video chatting just became a whole lot easier.

  • Calendar

    Ever missed out on the happening events on campus? Calendar is a two-way street giving Clubs, Departments & Regional Assocs a channel to publicize their upcoming events and ensuring you don't miss out on another minute of action on campus.

  • Services

    Make a poster, Learn to code or Master an instrument.Services provides you with the perfect platform to connect with experts

  • Problem Redressal System (P.R.S)

    Have you ever felt that your concerns have fallen on deaf ears? P.R.S provides you with the perfect opportunity to connect with your Student Representatives helping you voice your concerns and seek solutions in an efficient manner.

  • BITS Trade

    Ever faced a problem buying a trunk or selling your books? BITS Trade is your one stop destination for Buying and Selling.

  • Travel Planner

    Looking for company on your next trip to Delhi or Jaipur? Look no further, Travel Planner will put you in touch with fellow BITSians to make your journey easier.

FAQ

  • How can Clubs/Departments/Assocs publicize their events?

    There are two ways to publicize events on BITS Connect. Clubs/Departments/Assocs are provided with a separate account with special privileges to add events. Members of the concerned bodies can access the Calendar in order to add events. Posters/images can also be displayed on the front page by contatcting the administrator(s).

  • What is P.R.S. and how do I use it?

    The Problem Redressal System(P.R.S.) is a platform where you can post problems pertaining to your Bhawan, or the campus in general; This provides student representatives an opportunity to resolve these issues. P.R.S. is divided into several forums, each tackling issues related to the respective hostel. In addition, there is a Global forum for issues affecting campus welfare.

  • How do I make sure that my problem is addressed?

    Once you've posted your problem, other BITS Connect users can upvote it to echo your concerns(similar to Facebook's 'Like' feature). Problems shall be sorted by the number of upvotes they gather, making sure that the most important issues are tackled the earliest.

  • I am a Student Represenative, how can I convey to others that their problem has been addressed?

    Student Representives' accounts will be given special privileges to convey to people that the problem has been dealt with and give details about how it has been solved. This is how Student Representatives can stay in touch with the students and make the whole system effecient and transparent.

  • What are the services that can normally be offered on the website?

    Services are not offered but rather requested for. They could include requests for people profecient in various fields such as music, web/app developement, designing on photoshop/illustrator to give lessons or help out with any other NON-ACADEMIC assignment. Another feature that Clubs/Departments/Assocs can benefit from is freelancing their poster designs, content writing or any other such assignment.

  • How does BITS Travel work?

    BITS Travel is a portal where people travelling to prime desitnations like Delhi, Jaipur and Chandigarh can find others to travel with. Post your travel plans with a brief description including contact details, time of travel and then wait for a response.
